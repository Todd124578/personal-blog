# CMS Content Model

The visual CMS edits JSON files in `src/content`.

## Collections

- `settings`: site name, title, slogan, description, avatar, hero image, theme color, social links, community links.
- `posts`: articles with title, slug, status, date, cover, summary, category, tags, Markdown body, SEO title, SEO description, featured flag, reading time.
- `categories`: homepage and archive sections.
- `tags`: article tags.
- `projects`: portfolio and product work.
- `friends`: friend links.
- `media`: books and movies.
- `guestbook`: manually approved messages.
